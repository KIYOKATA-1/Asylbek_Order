Welcome to Astana Pediatric Clinic

At Astana Pediatric Clinic, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team of board-certified pediatricians and caring staff are committed to ensuring the health and well-being of your child.

About Us

Our clinic was founded with the mission of delivering high-quality medical care in a child-friendly environment. We offer a wide range of services, including well-child exams, immunizations, sick visits, developmental screenings, and more.

In addition to medical care, we strive to educate parents and empower families to make informed decisions about their children's health. We believe in fostering open communication and building lasting relationships with our patients and their families.

Latest News and Updates

New Vaccination Guidelines: The American Academy of Pediatrics has updated its vaccination guidelines to include recommendations for the COVID-19 vaccine for children ages 5 and older. Contact our clinic to schedule your child's vaccination appointment today.

Flu Season Preparedness: With flu season approaching, it's important to ensure your child is up-to-date on their flu vaccine. Our clinic offers flu shots for children of all ages. Schedule your appointment now to protect your family against the flu.

Back-to-School Checkups: As the new school year begins, don't forget to schedule your child's annual checkup. Our pediatricians can provide school physicals, sports physicals, and immunizations to help your child start the year healthy and ready to learn.

Healthy Eating Habits: Encouraging healthy eating habits from a young age is essential for your child's growth and development. Our pediatricians can offer guidance on nutrition and provide resources to help you make nutritious food choices for your family.

Parenting Tips: Looking for parenting advice? Check out our blog for helpful tips on everything from newborn care to managing temper tantrums. Our pediatricians are here to support you on your parenting journey.

Whether it's a routine checkup or treatment for an illness, our team is here to provide the personalized care your child deserves. Schedule an appointment with us today and experience the difference at Astana Pediatric Clinic.
