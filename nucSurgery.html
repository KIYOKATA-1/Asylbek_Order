Nuclear Surgery

Nuclear surgery, also known as nuclear medicine, is a specialized branch of medical imaging that utilizes small amounts of radioactive materials to diagnose and treat a variety of diseases. At Astana Wellness Center, our nuclear surgery department offers state-of-the-art imaging and treatment options using cutting-edge technology and a multidisciplinary approach.

Nuclear surgery involves the use of radioactive substances, called radiopharmaceuticals, which are injected, swallowed, or inhaled by patients. These substances emit gamma rays that can be detected by special cameras to produce images of the body's internal organs and tissues.

Our Services Include:

Diagnostic Imaging: We offer a wide range of nuclear imaging tests, including:

  • Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)
  • Positron Emission Tomography (PET)
  • Bone Scans
  • Thyroid Scans
  • Cardiac Stress Tests
  • Renal Scans
  • Gallium Scans

These imaging tests help diagnose conditions such as cancer, heart disease, thyroid disorders, neurological conditions, and more.

Therapeutic Procedures: In addition to diagnostic imaging, nuclear surgery can also be used for therapeutic purposes. Some of the treatments we offer include:

  • Radioactive Iodine Therapy for Thyroid Cancer
  • Radioembolization for Liver Cancer
  • Radiosynovectomy for Joint Pain
  • Radiopharmaceutical Therapy for Bone Pain

These treatments deliver targeted radiation directly to diseased tissues while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues, offering patients effective and minimally invasive treatment options.

Our team of board-certified nuclear medicine physicians, radiologists, technologists, and support staff are dedicated to providing exceptional care and ensuring the safety and comfort of our patients throughout their nuclear surgery experience.
