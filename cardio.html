Comprehensive Cardiovascular Care

At Astana Wellness Center, we understand the importance of cardiovascular health and are dedicated to providing comprehensive care for individuals with heart and vascular conditions. Our Comprehensive Cardiovascular Care program offers a wide range of services aimed at preventing, diagnosing, and treating cardiovascular diseases.

Our team of cardiologists, cardiovascular surgeons, nurses, and support staff work together to deliver personalized care tailored to each patient's unique needs. Whether you are seeking preventive services, diagnostic testing, or advanced treatment options, we are committed to helping you achieve optimal heart health and overall well-being.

Our Services Include:

Preventive Cardiology: Our preventive cardiology program focuses on identifying and managing risk factors for cardiovascular disease, such as high blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, obesity, and smoking. We provide personalized risk assessments, lifestyle counseling, and preventive interventions to help reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Diagnostic Testing: We offer a comprehensive range of diagnostic tests and imaging studies to evaluate heart and vascular function, including electrocardiography (ECG), echocardiography, stress testing, cardiac catheterization, CT angiography, and MRI scans. These tests help us diagnose heart conditions accurately and develop individualized treatment plans.

Interventional Cardiology: Our interventional cardiology team performs minimally invasive procedures to treat coronary artery disease, heart valve disorders, and peripheral vascular disease. Procedures include coronary angioplasty and stenting, transcatheter valve replacement, and peripheral artery interventions. These advanced techniques offer less pain, shorter recovery times, and improved outcomes compared to traditional open-heart surgery.

Cardiac Surgery: For patients requiring surgical intervention, our cardiovascular surgeons specialize in a wide range of procedures, including coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve repair or replacement, atrial fibrillation surgery, and aortic aneurysm repair. Our state-of-the-art operating rooms and experienced surgical teams ensure the highest quality of care and outcomes for our patients.

Heart Failure Management: Our heart failure program offers comprehensive care for patients with heart failure, including medical management, lifestyle modifications, device therapy (such as pacemakers and implantable cardioverter-defibrillators), and heart transplantation evaluation and coordination. Our goal is to optimize quality of life and improve outcomes for patients living with heart failure.

Cardiac Rehabilitation: Our cardiac rehabilitation program provides supervised exercise training, education, and support for patients recovering from heart surgery or experiencing cardiovascular conditions. Our multidisciplinary team helps patients regain strength, improve cardiovascular fitness, and adopt heart-healthy lifestyles to prevent future cardiac events.

Vascular Surgery: Our vascular surgeons specialize in treating conditions affecting the arteries and veins, including peripheral artery disease, carotid artery disease, abdominal aortic aneurysms, and venous disorders. We offer minimally invasive endovascular procedures as well as open surgical techniques to restore blood flow and prevent complications associated with vascular disease.

Electrophysiology: Our electrophysiology program focuses on diagnosing and treating heart rhythm disorders (arrhythmias) using advanced mapping and ablation techniques, implantable cardiac devices (such as pacemakers and defibrillators), and cardiac resynchronization therapy. Our electrophysiologists specialize in managing complex arrhythmias and offer innovative treatment options to improve heart rhythm control and quality of life.

Cardiovascular Research: As a leading academic medical center, we are actively involved in cardiovascular research and innovation. Our research programs aim to advance the understanding of heart and vascular diseases, develop new treatment strategies, and improve outcomes for patients. We participate in clinical trials and collaborate with national and international partners to translate scientific discoveries into clinical practice.

Whether you are seeking preventive care, diagnostic evaluation, or advanced treatment options for cardiovascular conditions, our team is here to provide compassionate care and support every step of the way. Your heart health is our top priority, and we are committed to helping you achieve a healthier, happier life.
