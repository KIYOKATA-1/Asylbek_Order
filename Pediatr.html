Pediatric Care

At Astana Wellness Center, we understand the unique healthcare needs of children. Our Pediatric Care program is dedicated to providing compassionate and comprehensive medical services for infants, children, and adolescents.

Our Pediatric Services Include:

Well-Baby and Well-Child Visits: We offer regular check-ups and developmental screenings to monitor your child's growth and development from infancy through adolescence. These visits are an opportunity to address any concerns, provide vaccinations, and offer guidance on nutrition and safety.

Acute and Chronic Illness Management: Our pediatricians are trained to diagnose and treat a wide range of acute and chronic conditions affecting children, including respiratory infections, ear infections, asthma, allergies, diabetes, and more. We work closely with families to develop personalized treatment plans and support children in achieving optimal health outcomes.

Immunizations: We follow the recommended vaccination schedule to protect children against serious infectious diseases. Our team educates parents about the importance of vaccinations and helps ensure that children receive all necessary vaccines for their age.

Growth and Development Monitoring: We track your child's physical, cognitive, and emotional development over time, identifying any potential concerns early and providing interventions as needed. Our goal is to support healthy growth and development in every child we care for.

Behavioral and Developmental Assessments: Our pediatricians are trained to evaluate and address behavioral and developmental issues in children, including attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), autism spectrum disorder (ASD), learning disabilities, and emotional concerns. We offer comprehensive assessments, referrals to specialists, and ongoing support for children and their families.

Nutritional Counseling: We provide guidance on healthy eating habits, breastfeeding support, and nutritional counseling for children with specific dietary needs or concerns. Our team works with families to promote healthy eating habits and address nutritional deficiencies or challenges.

Injury Prevention and Safety Education: We offer education and resources to help prevent common childhood injuries and accidents, including car seat safety, water safety, sports safety, and home safety. Our goal is to empower parents and caregivers with the knowledge and tools they need to keep their children safe and healthy.

Parent Education and Support: We believe in partnering with parents as active participants in their child's healthcare journey. Our pediatricians offer guidance, support, and resources to help parents navigate the challenges of parenthood, from newborn care to adolescence.

Whether your child needs a routine check-up, treatment for an illness, or guidance on parenting and child development, our team is here to provide expert care and support every step of the way.
