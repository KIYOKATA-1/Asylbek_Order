Gastrointestinal Health

Gastrointestinal health is crucial for overall well-being as it plays a vital role in digestion, nutrient absorption, and waste elimination. At Astana Wellness Center, we offer comprehensive services to address a wide range of gastrointestinal conditions and promote digestive wellness.

The gastrointestinal tract, also known as the digestive system, includes the mouth, esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine (colon), rectum, and anus. Common gastrointestinal issues include gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD), irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), gastritis, peptic ulcers, and colon polyps.

Our team of gastroenterologists, dietitians, and other specialists collaborate to provide personalized care and treatment plans tailored to each patient's needs. We utilize advanced diagnostic techniques and innovative treatment modalities to effectively manage gastrointestinal conditions and improve digestive health.

Our Services Include:

Diagnostic Procedures: We offer a comprehensive range of diagnostic procedures to evaluate gastrointestinal health, including upper endoscopy, colonoscopy, capsule endoscopy, and imaging studies such as CT scans and MRI.

Medical Management: Our gastroenterologists provide medical management for various gastrointestinal conditions, including prescribing medications, dietary modifications, and lifestyle changes to alleviate symptoms and improve overall digestive function.

Endoscopic Procedures: We perform a variety of endoscopic procedures to diagnose and treat gastrointestinal disorders, such as endoscopic mucosal resection (EMR), endoscopic ultrasound (EUS), and endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP).

Surgical Interventions: For certain gastrointestinal conditions that require surgical intervention, our experienced surgeons offer minimally invasive and traditional surgical procedures to provide relief and restore digestive health.

Nutritional Counseling: Our registered dietitians provide nutritional counseling and education to help patients manage gastrointestinal conditions through dietary modifications and optimize digestive health.

Functional Medicine Approach: We take a holistic approach to gastrointestinal health, addressing underlying factors such as gut microbiome imbalance, food sensitivities, and stress management to promote overall well-being and gastrointestinal function.

Whether you are experiencing symptoms such as abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea, or constipation, our team is here to provide compassionate care and support to help you achieve optimal gastrointestinal health.
